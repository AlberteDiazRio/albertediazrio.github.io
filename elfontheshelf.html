DESCRIPTION

The Elf on the Shelf: Christmas Heroes is a 3D action-adventure platformer developed by Outright Games, inspired by the beloved holiday franchise. The game invites players to explore festive environments, overcome obstacles, and complete dynamic challenges while embracing the magic and spirit of Christmas.

Throughout the adventure, players navigate varied thematic levels filled with traversal mechanics, environmental puzzles, and interactive elements designed to encourage exploration and discovery. The project focuses on accessible gameplay, polished level flow, and family-friendly design.

My role in the project was focused on Level Design. I was responsible for creating, prototyping, and developing complete levels from initial concept to final implementation. This included blockouts, gameplay iteration, encounter design, and ensuring a balanced difficulty curve aligned with the target audience.

In addition to gameplay design, I handled level lighting to enhance visual storytelling, mood, and player guidance. I worked on optimizing lighting setups to maintain performance across all supported platforms while reinforcing clarity, atmosphere, and readability within each environment.
