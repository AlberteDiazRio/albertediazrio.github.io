GAMEPLAY

DESCRIPTION

Truck Driver is a story-driven trucking simulator developed by SOEDESCO, focused on career progression, contract-based gameplay, and immersive vehicle handling mechanics.

My contribution to the project centered on cross-platform development and technical stabilization. I was responsible for bug fixing, performance optimization, and assisting in the porting process to PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X|S, and Nintendo Switch.

I worked on identifying and resolving gameplay and engine-level issues, addressing platform-specific constraints, optimizing memory usage, and ensuring compliance with console certification requirements. This role required close attention to hardware limitations, build validation processes, and maintaining feature parity across all supported platforms.
